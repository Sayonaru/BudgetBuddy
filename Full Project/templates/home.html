What do we do?

Welcome to our finance tracker platform designed for both universities and students. Our platform offers essential features for university business finance, including an easy-to-read dashboard, summary reports, and categorization of expenses, among others. For students, we provide basic income and expense tracking, budget and savings goal setting, subscription tracking, and notifications when goals are met or expenses exceed income. We offer different account types and subscription options, including a student premium account with additional features. Our goal is to help universities and students track their finances and achieve their financial goals efficiently and effectively.

What Do We Offer?

Our finance tracker platform provides a user-friendly, all-in-one solution for businesses and university students to track and manage their finances. The platform offers customizable categories for expenses and income, forecasting tools, and in-app support to provide users with help and advice as needed. By using the platform, businesses and students can gain more control over their finances and make better-informed financial decisions.
